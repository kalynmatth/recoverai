Athletic trainer partnerships
B2BHigh LTVFast growth
›
Approach certified ATCs at colleges and pro sports teams. One ATC prescribing your app to a roster of 50 athletes is 50 high-intent users acquired in a single conversation. Build a referral incentive and track conversions per ATC partner.
Short-form video
TikTokInstagramYouTube Shorts
›
Post 30-second "day X of recovery" exercise demos on TikTok and Reels. Injured athletes actively search this content. Link bio to app download. Low production cost, high reach — a single viral clip can drive thousands of installs.

Viral loopZero costHigh intent
›
Let users share recovery milestones as branded cards to social media. "Day 30 — ACL recovery complete" posts are authentic user-generated content that drives installs from highly motivated audiences who relate to the journey.
Insurance partnerships
EnterpriseLong-termHigh revenue

Many insurers will cover or subsidize a clinically-backed recovery app when outcomes data shows reduced re-injury rates. A single insurance partnership puts RecoverAI in front of millions of members with zero acquisition cost per user.
